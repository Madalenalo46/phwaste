Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster do I need?

It depends on your project! Our 10 yard dumpster is great for small projects like a garage cleanout. The 20 yard dumpster is our most versatile size, perfect for renovations or large cleanouts. For massive jobs, our 30 yard dumpster is the way to go. Give us a call, and we can help you choose the perfect roll off dumpster size.

What can I put in my roll off dumpster?

You can toss most common household and construction debris, including furniture, wood, drywall, roofing shingles, yard waste, and appliances.

How do I know what I can and can't throw away?

You can dispose of most non-hazardous waste, like furniture, wood, drywall, yard debris, and appliances. However, items like chemicals, paint, oils, asbestos, and batteries are prohibited. When in doubt, give our team a call!.

Can I rent a dumpster for just yard waste?

Absolutely! We offer yard waste dumpster rentals perfect for large landscaping projects, storm cleanup, and seasonal clearing. Just be sure not to mix yard waste with other types of debris.

Can I mix yard waste with other household junk?

In most cases, yes! You can mix general household debris, furniture, and yard waste in the same roll off dumpster container. The only major exceptions are prohibited hazardous materials.

How long should I rent the dumpster for my remodel?

Most of our clients rent a roll off dumpster for the duration of their demolition phase, typically 5-7 days. We offer flexible rental periods to match your project's timeline, whether it's a quick weekend demo or a longer, phased renovation.
